Peter Aletor

Group Managing Director/Chief Executive Officer

Peter Aletor is a professional accountant and a pioneer staff of Capital Express Securities Limited where he became the company’s Managing Director.

He established Apel Asset Limited in 2008 and has continued to carry out big-ticket transactions for individual and corporate clients. He is an Authorized Dealing Clerk of The Nigerian Exchange Group and a Fellow of the Chartered Institute of Stockbrokers.

He has about 30 years of experience as a Chartered Stockbroker on the floor of The Nigerian Exchange Group (NGX).

He is the Group Managing Director of Apel, overseeing the entire organization.

He is an alumnus of Lagos Business School (LBS) and has attended the Reimagining Strategy Course at Harvard Business School.

LOLA IYINOLAKAN

Managing Director, Apel Capital & Trust

She has over 18 years of work experience across Legal Practice and the Financial Services sector.

She began her career in the financial services sector at Zenith Bank Plc in 2008 after a short stint in a Law Firm. She was thereafter seconded to Zenith Trustees and later moved to Coronation Trustees Limited where she was the Managing Director until July 2020.

She holds a Bachelor of Law degree, LL.B. (Hons) from the Olabisi Onabanjo University, Ogun State, and a Masters of Law degree, LL.M from the University of Lagos. She is a member of the Nigerian Bar Association and an Associate member of the Institute of Chartered Secretaries & Administrators, London.

Omolola is currently the Managing Director of Apel Capital & Trust Limited and she is responsible for setting the strategic direction for the growth of the Firm.

TEMITOPE ADEOSUN

Managing Director, Apel Asset Limited

Temitope has distinguished investment banking experience spanning advisory, portfolio management, proprietary trading, market making, and relationship management. She possesses over twenty-three years of professional experience.

Currently, Temitope serves as the Managing Director of Apel Asset Limited. She currently oversees the company’s daily business operations, directing all the resources to achieve strategic growth plans.

Prior to joining Capital Bancorp, Temitope was the MD/CEO of Bancorp Securities Limited, a subsidiary of Capital Bancorp PLC, and MD/CEO of FBNQuest Securities Limited, the securities trading arm/subsidiary of FBNQuest Merchant Bank. She was responsible for managing the Company’s diverse business activities and leading the team.

Temitope is a Council member of the Chartered Institute of Stockbrokers and served as a member of the National Association of Securities Dealers (NASD) as an Audit Committee member between 2016 - 2018. She holds an MBA in Financial Management, BSc in Finance, and HND in Business Administration.

CHIOMA SIDESO

Director

She is a senior corporate and financial services executive with more than 23 years’ experience of strategic leadership across the oil and gas, banking, and insurance sectors. She has led several international companies as CEO and is also an accomplished Non-Executive Director, with a strong track record of delivering change. She was instrumental in signing Nigeria’s first ever bancassurance deal and introduced ground-breaking market segmentation techniques which contributed to a huge increase in the corporate client base. Previously, she was an Executive Director for the same firm from 2004 to 2006, having originally started her career in London in 1999. She has over time continued to support young people and women across Africa, particularly in their journeys towards financial independence. She enjoys sharing her technical and commercial knowledge to raise performance and capabilities. Chioma Sideso brings thought leadership and energy to top management. She is dedicated to making a positive impact, reducing risk, strengthening efficiencies, and facilitating multiple income streams.
